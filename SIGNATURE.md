# Luna Llena Unisex Beauty Salon — SIGNATURE

## Brand truth
"Luna Llena" is Spanish for **Full Moon**. The salon sits at 351 Main St, City of
Orange NJ, a Spanish-speaking block (Orange Unisex Nail is its literal neighbor at
347). Unisex, walk-ins + appointments, cash + card. Modest public footprint, no
website, no published hours, weak directory rating — so we do NOT lead with reviews
or invented hours. We lead with the NAME: the full moon, and "se ve bien bajo
cualquier luz" / "you look good under any light."

## The ONE concept (brand-truth x technique)
**Moonlight.** A salon named for the full moon, art-directed as a calm coastal-night
beauty house. The signature is a living **full-moon light source**: a luminous
gradient orb (teal-to-moonlight) that breathes and casts a soft tidal glow over a
**masonry gallery hero** of real salon work. The moon is the hero's light; the
gallery is what that light reveals. Coastal-clean, not pink-floral, not generic
discount-shop — the deliberate anti-cliché for a Main St unisex salon.

## Signature moment (the screenshot)
The **Luna hero**: a masonry wall of salon-work tiles behind a slow-breathing
full-moon orb. As you scroll, the moon drifts and its glow rakes across the tiles
(parallax + radial-glow position coupled to scroll), like moonlight moving over
water. The masonry tiles rise in on staggered reveal. ONE confident move:
moonlight over the work. Degrades to a static glowing moon + static masonry under
prefers-reduced-motion. Not WebGL — pure CSS/transform + Framer, fast and graceful.

## Lane (locked, honored)
- Accent **#14868C** (coastal teal) — used as the moon's cool edge + CTAs + rules.
- Display font **Supreme** (Fontshare) — the whole voice, paired with a quiet serif
  for Spanish flourish lines.
- Layout **masonry gallery hero** — the tide-wall of work, the signature container.
- Mood **coastal-clean** — moonlight whites, sand neutral, deep teal-night, lots of
  air. Calm, expensive, unmistakably a beauty house.
